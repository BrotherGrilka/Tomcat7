Competitive Camps - For the advanced player, this camp provides an opportunity for the ambitious player to prepare for the next level. Technique & mobility are the focal points of this camp. Each level an athlete advances to the demands are greater. We will help prepare athletes in an intensive, competitive environment.

4-A-Side League Play - 4 nights, 2 games per night. Games start on the half hour. League play provides a free flowing competitive environment for players to challenge themselves. Each game is supervised by staff members who will coach & provide instruction following each game.
